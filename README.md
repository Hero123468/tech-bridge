---

## What Tech Bridge Does

Tech Bridge connects early-career developers with small businesses through real, scoped tasks — completed live on the web with no shortcuts and no escape hatch. Every task follows a structured documentation model:

- **The Break** — What happened, and what was your cognitive state?
- **The Investigation** — What logic did you use to isolate the variable?
- **The Fix** — What was the technical solution, and what did you learn?
- **The Proof** — Verifiable evidence that the system is now functional.

For developers, you get a foot in the door and a documented record of real judgment. For businesses, you get a chance to advertise, fix a real issue, and onboard talent through action — not resumes. For both sides: no ghosting, clear expectations, and a chance to build rapport.

---

## Why This Is Different

This is not a job board. This is a reputation engine.

AI has made it nearly impossible to separate fake from real. If hiring managers are searching for people who "use sound judgment" over people who claim they "can build" — Tech Bridge is the answer to that job listing.

Our answer is simple, yet deep: **I know S.T.U.F.F.**

> Software, Typed, Understood, Functional, Factored.

Developers who complete tasks successfully earn a digital credential showing exactly what they did, how they thought, and that their solution works — proof of work, not AI resume slush. Symmetrical accountability means companies are on the record too. Ghost a developer? That's documented. Both sides are reported to the BBB for verified misconduct. No one hides here.

## How to Contribute

We don't just want validation. We are openly asking for critique — especially from senior developers and prospective businesses.

Known open questions we need help with:
- What are the legal implications of recording someone live online during a task?
- What should a verified work entry actually contain?
- How should the BBB reporting pipeline be structured?
 
Open an issue labeled `critique`, `legal-question`, or `lived-experience` and tell us what you know.

## Proposed Tech Stack

Currently leaning toward Python full stack, though open to JavaScript. Stack discussion is an active conversation — open an issue labeled `stack-discussion` if you have a strong case either way.

**Early technical direction (community input):**
- Auth system for Admin and User roles, tied into the site with a persistent login component visible on every page (top right corner, following standard UX conventions)
- Reusable component architecture following the DRY principle — components, hooks, navbars, and login UI should never be repeated
- Open to routing and framework discussion — no direction is locked in yet

## Contributors & Credits

Tech Bridge is built on the belief that credit is non-negotiable. If you contribute, you get acknowledged — on your terms.

**"No Black Box" feature concept** — the principle that task completion must happen live, on the web, with no ability to close the tab or hide the process — was contributed by a community member who prefers to remain uncredited at this stage. This feature is foundational to Tech Bridge's proof-of-work philosophy.

*Test supervision, borrowed libraries, and future contributors will be credited in footnotes as the project develops. If you contribute and prefer to remain anonymous, your contribution is still documented internally and honored. No one disappears from this project's history.*
